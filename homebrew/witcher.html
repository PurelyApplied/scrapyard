### Witcher Medallion Upon completion of your training as a witcher, you are awarded with a *Witcher Medallion* fashioned into the symbol of your school. Foremost, the medallion functions as proof of your training and station as a witcher. Additionally, when worn by a witcher, the medallion vibrates in the presence of magic. \page ### Deflect Misiles *[Draft notes: Mostly a reskin of the Monk's **Deflect Missiles**, except that you don't attack with the caught item]* Starting at the 3rd level, you can use your reaction to deflect or catch the missile when you are hit by a ranged weapon attack while you are wielding one of your witcher weapons. When you do so, the damage you take from the attack is reduced by 1d10 + your Dexterity modifier + your witcher level. *[Draft note: Attack, sign, or none? The number of attacks increases to three when you reach 11th level in this class and to four when you reach 20th level in this class. ### Careful Preparation Beginning at the 4th level, a witcher may prepare for a specific foe. During a short or long rest, a witcher may use witcher's tools to create and apply special oils to their witcher weapons. The oil doubles the Witcher Weapon Damage Bonus to one creature type. A different type of oil may be applied to each witcher weapon, but only one oil may effect a witcher weapon at a time. ### Emotionally Numb *[Draft note:] Reskin of level 7 Monk **Stillness of Mind**]* Through the combination of your progressing mutations and your difficult life, you have gained full control of what little emotion you yet feel.
